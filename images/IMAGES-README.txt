AQUACRAFT PLUMBING — IMAGE PLACEHOLDERS
========================================

Drop your real photos into this folder and update index.html accordingly.

Suggested files:
  hero.jpg              — Hero banner (landscape, 1600x900 min). Plumber working, clean pipes, water, Sonoma County scene.
  about.jpg             — About section (portrait or square). Photo of Ramzy, the team, or a truck.
  service-waterheater.jpg  — For a water heater service image (optional)
  service-drain.jpg        — For a drain cleaning image (optional)
  service-leak.jpg         — For a leak detection image (optional)
  og-image.jpg             — Open Graph share image (1200x630). Can reuse hero.

Current photos are loaded from Unsplash (royalty-free, no watermark).
Replace them any time by putting a file here and updating the src="" in index.html.

DO NOT use AI-generated images or images with watermarks.
